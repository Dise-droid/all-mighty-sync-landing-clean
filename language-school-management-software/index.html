Language School Management Software

All Mighty Sync is club management software built for language schools and tutoring centers. It replaces the group chats, spreadsheets and paper attendance sheets most schools run on with one platform covering lesson attendance, level progression, parent communication and payments. Instructors monitor speaking, writing, listening and reading levels per student, track textbook and module progress, and log verbal assessments and vocabulary benchmarks. Administrators manage classes, levels and membership payments from one dashboard.
