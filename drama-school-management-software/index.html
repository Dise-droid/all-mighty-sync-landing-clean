Drama School Management Software

All Mighty Sync is club management software built for drama schools and acting academies. It replaces the group chats, spreadsheets and paper attendance sheets most schools run on with one platform covering rehearsal attendance, auditions, productions, student portfolios and payments. Instructors assess scene work, monologue delivery, improvisation and stage confidence, and record audition results and performance history - building an audition-ready portfolio every student can carry with them. Administrators manage classes, rehearsal schedules and membership payments from one dashboard. It's available as a mobile app for instructors, parents and students, alongside a web dashboard for administrators.
