Art School Management Software

All Mighty Sync is club management software built for art schools, studios and creative education programs. It replaces the group chats, spreadsheets and paper attendance sheets most programs run on with one platform covering class attendance, creative project tracking, exhibitions and payments. Instructors log creative works by date and medium, track technique development and project completion, and record exhibition results and teacher critique notes. Administrators manage classes and membership payments from one dashboard.
