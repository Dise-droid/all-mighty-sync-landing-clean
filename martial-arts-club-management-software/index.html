Martial Arts Club Management Software

All Mighty Sync is club management software built for martial arts clubs, dojos and multi-discipline academies. It replaces the group chats, spreadsheets and paper attendance sheets most clubs run on with one platform covering training attendance, belt and grade progression, competition results and payments. Instructors monitor belt and grade progression, sparring evaluations, technique ratings per category, competition results and physical conditioning benchmarks. Administrators manage classes, groups and membership payments from one dashboard. It's available as a mobile app for instructors, parents and students, alongside a web dashboard for administrators.
