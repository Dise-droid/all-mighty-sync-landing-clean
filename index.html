ONE ECOSYSTEM

One account. Every organization. Everything connected.

All Mighty Sync is not a tool you use inside one school. It is the platform you carry with you.

A parent with two children

One child in a football academy. Another in a dance school. Two organizations. One account. One notification center. Both portfolios. Both payments. Always together.

A teacher working in two places

Teaches drama on Tuesdays and a language school on Thursdays. Two organizations. One login. Their groups, schedules, and student lists for both - one device.

A student who changes schools

Moves from one football club to another. Their benchmark history, assessments, and development portfolio transfer with them - because it belongs to them, not to the old club.

An organization with multiple disciplines

A private academy running football, dance, and music simultaneously. Each discipline has its own groups, portfolio structure, and terminology. All managed from one admin account.

Everyone sees exactly what they are allowed to see. Nothing more. Nothing crosses between organizations. But the experience is seamless - because you only ever need one account.

YOUR IDENTITY

All Mighty Sync provides the technology. Your organization keeps its identity.

When your members open the app, they see your organization. Your name. Your structure. Your terminology. If you call your groups "squads" - the app says squads. If your evaluation categories have specific names - they appear exactly as you define them.

Every organization on All Mighty Sync is completely distinct. Your disciplines, your schedules, your payment setup - all configured exactly for you. You are not adapting to a generic platform. The platform is adapting to you.

We build and maintain the technology. You focus on building something great.

GETTING STARTED

From first call to fully operational - faster than you expect.

No IT department required. Our team walks you through it once, and then you're running on your own.

01

Meet with our team

We learn how your organization works, which disciplines you run, and what structure makes sense for you. We agree on the setup approach together.

02

We set up your organization

Your administrator receives access and guidance to configure groups, disciplines, terminology, schedules, and permissions. We are available throughout.

03

Invite your people

Invite teachers, students and parents all at once - with a single click. Everyone receives an email invitation and joins an organization that's already structured and ready. They open the app and everything is there.

04

Operate independently

Day-to-day, your organization runs through the platform. Our team remains available if you need us, but the admin controls everything.

THE TEAM

All Mighty Sync was built by people who lived the problem.

Anja Ćurković and Edis Bilić created All Mighty Sync after spending years watching the same scene repeat itself: outstanding coaches and teachers spending hours every week on WhatsApp threads, Excel files, paper lists and phone calls.

Hours that weren't spent in the gym. Hours that weren't spent in the studio. Hours that weren't spent developing students.

The technology to solve this problem already existed. What didn't exist was a platform that understood how extracurricular organizations actually work - so they built it.
