Music School Management Software

All Mighty Sync is club management software built for music schools, whether teaching individual lessons, ensembles or choirs. It replaces the group chats, spreadsheets and paper attendance sheets most schools run on with one platform covering lesson attendance, grade and repertoire progression, parent communication and payments. Instructors track grade and level progression, repertoire completion, theory knowledge and performance recordings, and link sessions to specific pieces or programs. Administrators manage lessons, ensembles and membership payments from one dashboard. It's available as a mobile app for instructors, parents and students, alongside a web dashboard for administrators.
